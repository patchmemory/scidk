What are Labels?

Labels define the types of nodes in your Neo4j graph. Each label specifies a set of properties, data types, and validation rules. Labels enable schema enforcement, indexing, and structured querying across your knowledge graph. When a label is pushed to Neo4j, its constraints and indexes are created in the database.
