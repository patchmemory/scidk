Labels

Label definitions provide schema structure for your graph by defining custom node types with properties, constraints, and relationships.

What are Labels?

Labels define the types of nodes in your Neo4j graph. Each label specifies a set of properties, data types, and validation rules. Labels enable schema enforcement, indexing, and structured querying across your knowledge graph.

Authoring & Management

Label definitions are created, edited, and managed on the Labels page.
