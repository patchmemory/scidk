What are Analyses?

Analysis modules are specialized scripts (Cypher queries or Python code) that read from the graph, perform computations, and write results back as nodes or properties. They enable reproducible analytical workflows, automated reporting, and data quality checks. Analyses run on-demand and store their output for review.
